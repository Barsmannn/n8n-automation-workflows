# n8n Automation Workflows

Ready-to-deploy n8n workflows for common business automation needs. Import the JSON, add your credentials, run.

## Workflows Included

### 1. CRM → Slack Lead Notifier
New contact in HubSpot/Pipedrive → instant Slack message to sales team
**Setup time:** 5 minutes

### 2. Form → CRM → Email Sequence
Typeform/Tally submission → adds to CRM → triggers email sequence in Mailchimp
**Setup time:** 10 minutes

### 3. Invoice Auto-Follow-Up
Unpaid invoice after 7 days → automatic follow-up email → Slack alert to owner
**Setup time:** 8 minutes

## How to Import
1. Open your n8n instance
2. Click **Import from file**
3. Select the `.json` workflow file
4. Add your credentials
5. Activate
